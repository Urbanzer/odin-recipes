Lasagna

Delicious looking lasagna

Every now and then this legendary food is to be craved!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Salt and pepper
  • Meat

    • Steps

    1. Take your first sheet of pasta and put it on a baking pan.
    2. Dress it with meat and ketchup.
    3. Put some cheese on it.
    4. Repeat above steps atleast three times.
    5. Put the stuffed baking pan into an oven at 200 degrees celsius for a 40 minute duration.
